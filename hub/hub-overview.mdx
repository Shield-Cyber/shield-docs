title: "Hub"
description: "The Hub provides a central place to perform additional actions in the platform.  These actions are grouped into bringing data into the platform (Collect Data), processing (Manage Settings) and taking data out of the platform (Deliver Results)."
---

Open the Hub by clicking the Hub button in the top left corner.  Close the Hub by clicking the X in the top right corner or clicking outside of the Hub.
