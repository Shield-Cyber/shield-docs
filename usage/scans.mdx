title: "Scans"
description: "The Scans module will allow users to create and control all network-based scanning of the target external attack surface assets and internal network assets."
---

The **Scanning** functionality is dedicated to creating, configuring, and managing network-based scans for deployed external and internal scanners that will target an organization's infrastructure and attempt to identify vulnerabilities.

Each subscription will come pre-configured with the ability to scan externally and setup with a dedicated scanner.

### Scan Creation

Users have the ability to create and configure network-based vulnerability scans against their external and internal network asset targets.
