### Most Vulnerable Products

The most vulnerable products are shown based on the number and severity of the vulnerabilities detected.  The vulnerabilities, versions, subscriptions and assets are rolled up by default.  To break down by one or more of these dimensions, click on its respective number.  As an example, to break down by versions click on a version count.  To drill down into an individual product, click on its name.

<Frame type="glass">
  <img src="/images/products-by-version.png" alt="Products by version" />
</Frame>

### Invididual Product

When an individual product is selected, the most vulnerable subscriptions and most vulnerable versions within the product will be shown with additional details.  Similar to above, dimensions are rolled up by default.  To break down by one or more of the dimensions, click on its respective number.  To filter on a specific value, click on its name.
